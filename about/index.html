About Angela

Wed, Apr 9, 2014

It was a sunny spring afternoon. We were sitting in a coffeeshop filled with idle chatter. Across from me sat the man who kickstarted my career by giving me my first full time job. A lot has happened since the day we first met. The conversation had suddenly taken a deep turn. I was talking about that lingering feeling of doubt that lives with me. The doubt that what I’ll do and the things I’ll conquer will never be enough - that I will always want more.

Everyone sees the fire and the ambition, but they don’t see the fear that drives me. I don’t know how not chase these dreams. Will I find a journey that has meaning to me, and to others, I wonder? If my life ended tomorrow, would I have left an impact on anyone? Would I be remembered?

As I was trying to articulate this, there was this moment where he looked at me, head slightly tilted. A heavy pause, as the door swung open and the shop engulfed the newest visitors in the heady aroma of ground coffee. And he asked me, “Angela, are you searching for significance?”“

The question has stuck in my head since. I had never quite heard it put that way. But it is the question I am chasing after. The answer I want to find. Is this the calling that people speak of? When they find work that sustains the soul? Who am I? What do I really want? What greatness am I meant for in this world? I don’t know yet.

I hope to find out.

My name is Angela.

This is my hybrid portfolio-blog filled with data things and the thoughts that fill my head and keep me up at night. Hope you enjoy the read!
